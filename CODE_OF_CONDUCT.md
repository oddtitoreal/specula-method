# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The Specula Method community is committed to providing a welcoming, intellectually honest, and ethically grounded space for practitioners, researchers, and organizations exploring speculative branding.

## Our Standards

**We value:**
- Intellectual rigor and honesty about limitations
- Sharing failures and learnings, not just successes
- Diverse perspectives and constructive critique
- Respect for confidentiality and anonymization practices
- Commitment to responsible AI and ethical foresight

**We don't tolerate:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary behavior
- Misrepresentation of the method or case outcomes
- Sharing of confidential client information
- Use of the framework for manipulative or extractive practices
- Academic dishonesty or plagiarism

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to [tito@marcolivi.it]. All reports reviewed confidentially. Consequences range from warning to permanent ban depending on severity.

## Attribution

When using Specula Method in publications, presentations, or commercial work, please attribute appropriately and link to this repository.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces: GitHub issues, discussions, events, and any space representing the Specula community.

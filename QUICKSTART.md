# Quick Start

**You're working with a team that knows what it values but can't yet say what kind of future it wants to build toward.**

This guide gets you from zero to a first scenario seed in under 30 minutes. No frameworks to learn first. Start here, go deeper later.

## Before you start

You need:
- A client or team willing to talk about the future, not just the present
- The [Method Agent configuration](./agent/specula-method-agent.md) (optional but recommended)
- 30–90 minutes of facilitated conversation

## Step 1 — Open with the right question (5 min)

Don't start with "what do you do". Start with:

> "If this organization were perfectly coherent with its values in 2035 — what would it look like?"

Note what they say. Contradictions are data, not problems.

## Step 2 — Run Phase 1: Scenario Generation (20 min)

Ask three questions, in sequence:

1. What sector and time horizon are we working with?
2. Which variable today scares or excites you most?
3. What's a future you'd be proud to have contributed to?

Capture answers in the session deliverable format.
→ See [session_example_01.md](./examples/session_example_01.md) and [session_example_02.md](./examples/session_example_02.md)

## Step 3 — Identify what stays and what the brand refuses (10 min)

From the session, extract:
- Repeated words or metaphors — these are value signals
- What they *won't* do — these are refusal candidates
- Contradictions between stated values and current behavior — these are identity tensions

This primes Phase 2 (Brand Archeology).

## A real example

*Il posto delle fragole* is a community space in Pesaro built by four partner organizations for young people. It started with three co-design workshops and no coherent identity. The Specula Method turned that raw material into a governance system for brand identity — from values to visual system to operational rules.

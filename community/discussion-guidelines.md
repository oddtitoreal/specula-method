# Discussion Guidelines

## Purpose

GitHub Discussions for Specula Method is for:
- Practical questions about applying the framework
- Sharing learnings and adaptations
- Peer support among practitioners
- Research collaboration
- Proposing improvements to templates or ethical framework

## Not For

- Marketing or self-promotion
- Client solicitation
- Sharing confidential information
- Theoretical debate without practical grounding
- Off-topic discussions

## Categories

**Q&A**: Specific questions about method application  
**Show and Tell**: Share (anonymized) applications and learnings  
**Ideas**: Propose framework improvements  
**Research**: Academic partnerships and validation studies  
**Events**: Meetups, workshops, conferences

## Best Practices

### When Asking Questions
- Provide context (which phase, what challenge)
- Explain what you've already tried
- Be specific about where you're stuck

### When Sharing Learnings
- Anonymize thoroughly
- Focus on method, not just outcomes
- Include failures and challenges
- Explain adaptations you made

### When Proposing Changes
- Ground in real-world application
- Explain the problem being solved
- Consider backward compatibility
- Reference existing templates or docs

## Response Times

- Maintainers aim to respond within 1 week
- Community members encouraged to help each other
- Complex questions may take longer

## Moderation

Discussions are lightly moderated to maintain focus and respect. See [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) for standards.
